Brazilian Barbecue

Ingredients:

  • Pre-made pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Sliced calabresa sausage
  • Dried oregano

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven according to the instructions on the dough package.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a border for the crust.
  4. Distribute shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add sliced calabresa sausage over the cheese.
  6. Sprinkle oregano over the pizza.
  7. Bake the pizza in the oven for the time indicated on the dough package, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  8. Remove from the oven, slice, and serve.
