Hamburger

Ingredients:

  • Hamburger bun
  • Beef patty
  • Cheese (mozzarella or cheddar)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices (optional)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Season the patty: Mix ground beef with salt and pepper. Form into patties slightly larger than the bun.
  2. Cook the patty: Grill or pan-fry the patty to your desired doneness. Add cheese on top during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  3. Toast the bun: Lightly toast the cut sides of the hamburger bun.
  4. Assemble the burger: Spread mayonnaise on the bottom bun.
  5. Add lettuce, tomato, onion (if using), and the patty with cheese. Top with ketchup and mustard (if using). Cover with the top bun.
  6. Serve immediately: Enjoy your delicious Burger!
