Brazilian Barbecue

Ingredients:

  • Quality meat
  • Coarse Salt

Steps:

  1. Salt the meat: Generously coat the beef with coarse sea salt.Don't be shy, we're not making delicate appetizers here.
  2. Grill it: Place the salted meat on a hot grill. Cook until it's perfectly seared on the outside and juicy on the inside. No need for fancy thermometers or timers, just trust your instincts (and maybe a little peek to check).
  3. Rest and slice: Let the meat rest for a few minutes before slicing against the grain. This is crucial for maximum tenderness and flavor.
  4. Devour: Serve immediately and enjoy the pure, unadulterated taste of perfectly grilled beef. No sauces, no sides, just the meat..
