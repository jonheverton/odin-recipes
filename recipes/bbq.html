Brazilian Barbecue

Want to master Brazilian BBQ? Easy. Just grab some top-quality meat, season it with nothing but salt

Ingredients:

  • Quality meat
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Generously season the meat with coarse salt
  2. Grill over open flames or hot coals, turning occasionally until it's perfectly charred on the outside and juicy on the inside. Slice, serve, and watch it disappear.
